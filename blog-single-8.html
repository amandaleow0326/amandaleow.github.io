What is GSF Connect?

GSF Connect aims to target teen girls in years 7-12 to challenge how they think, what they strive for, and why they want it. It is a platform that provides a wide range of interactive opportunities, from monthly meet-up with other like-minding girls to company excursions to see behind the curtains of some of the most exciting brands. Besides, there are exclusive self-development events like workshops and camps catered for the experience. GSF Connect also act as a video gallery of inspirational, informational and insightful videos that hosts over 100 videos, updated each week.

GSF Before

GSF Connect aims to target teen girls in years 7-12, however the page was not attractive to the eyeballs of teen girls. It was more inclined to an informative page that the persona of teen girls does not resonate with but whom are more inclined to engage with imagery visuals.

UX Design Processes

User Persona

Creating personas helps the designer and client understand the users' needs, experiences, behaviour and goals to better cater to their needs accurately. In this case, GSF Connect aims to target the teen girls, parents and educators through their webpage.

Lo-fi wireframe

Lo-fi wireframes are basic representations of the concepts that would help the client visualise and validate concept and user flows.

User journey mapping

User journey mapping is a visualisation of the process that the user goes through in order to perform a certain goal of action.
